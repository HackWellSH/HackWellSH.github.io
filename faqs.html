FAQ

Answering all the burning questions.

On to the questions!

Question 1 - What is a hackathon?

Hackathons are in-person coding events where teenagers come together to learn new skills, create fun projects, and make memories. There's also food, snacks, and drinks to fuel your creativity. Instead of hacking bank accounts like you hear in the news, you'll build something meaningful to you.

Question 2 - Who can participate?

We're inviting all students from year 7 - year 13 to participate in HackWell2023 for a small fee of 250RMB per participant.

Question 3 - Do I need to know how to code?

No! This is a STEM hackathon, including various different elements of STEM, not just computer science.

Question 4 - What do I need to bring?

Your Computer Science/ICT teacher will have a packing list for the days.

Question 5 - What will be provided?

Dinner will be provided for Saturday, but on Saturday and Sunday lunchtime you will be allowed to go out and eat by yourselves. Snacks will also be provided.

Question 6 - How do the groups work?

Groups of 2-4 will be announced near the date of the event.
