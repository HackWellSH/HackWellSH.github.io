About

HackWell is the first ever Wellington hackathon competition across the Wellington college schools in China, hosted by the pupils in Wellington College International Shanghai. We are hoping that this competition will be able to be a permenant, long lasting event that will continue to be held even after our graduation.

Unlike to conventional image of hackathons only being a computer science related competition, aspects of various other STEM subjects were also integrated within the competition as we wish to allow the participants to not only show their abilities but also obtain new knowledge and experiences from various other participants with different strengths and abilities.

We welcome everyone who is willing to join our HackWell and are looking forward to seeing you at the competition! Parents are welcome to join on the final day to attend the projects showcase and closing ceremony.

If you wish to sign up, please contact your Computer Science/ICT teacher. There is a 250RMB participation fee per person. The deadline for signing up is the 14th of November.
