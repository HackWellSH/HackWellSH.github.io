Prompts

release date: 25.11.2023 8:45:00am

Scenario 1 - Perfect Launch Angle

Ever found yourself frustrated in Angry Birds, unable to hit those evil pigs with your feathered friends? Don’t worry! By integrating mathematics, physics, and computer science, we can uncover the ultimate solution. ​ Utilize your technological skills to construct a model that determines the best launch angle for hitting the pigs in the game. Factors such as the bird’s velocity, the pig’s position and the distance should be considered. With your solution, players can nail this game easily!

Scenario 2 - Airplanes

Aeroplanes, serving as crucial modes of transportation, provide us with unparalleled convenience in our daily lives. However, certain circumstances such as adverse weather conditions can lead to delays, disrupting our travel plans. ​ Utilize your technological skills to construct a mathematical model that estimates the probability of flight delays . Your solution should consider various factors, offering a comprehensive approach to address and predict potential disruptions in air travel.​

Scenario 3 - Particle Collision

Chemistry is the study of chemical elements and different types of chemical reactions between them. A visualised platform will significantly benefit students and researchers in exploring the reactions under different conditions. ​ Please develop a particle collision simulator focusing on the dynamic interplay of different reactants. Consider customizable parameters such as reactant concentrations, temperature, and reaction pathways etc.​ ​

Scenario 4 - Disability Hazards

Millions of individuals around the world navigate their daily lives with either visual or hearing disabilities, facing unique challenges that the rest of us may take for granted. ​ Please design a recognition AI that outputs sounds or vibrations when certain sorts of danger are detected for people with sight disabilities or hearing disabilities preventing any potential hazards.​ ​

Scenario 5 - Informational Education

In the crowded network, if the resources required during studies can be well organised in one platform, it will greatly enhance our learning efficiency. ​ Please develop an educational app, where users can find different types of information needed about certain subjects. Think about the problem you encountered during your studies and the resources you used for revision or studying. Probably this will be a good start before programming.​ ​

Scenario 6 - AID for Art

AI is here with us and its pretty much becoming a household name. However, to include people with disabilities in this arena, much more technologies are needed to assist them. ​ Think of a way in which disabled artists whether they are songwriters, directors, singers, digital artists, social media influencers or any other art domain could be included in the AI digital platform by creating appropriate technologies to assist them live full lives like all of us.​ ​

Scenario 7 - Sustainable City

Shanghai is our home and how we protect it determines its future. Often it is said that we borrow the earth from our grandchildren. Meaning we must live sustainably. ​ Having recovered from the pandemic, what technologies can we develop to ensure city dwellers adopt sustainable life styles in their homes?​ ​

Scenario 8 - Pet Locator

We all love pets, often they stray out of our homes and it’s a pain trying to locate them. ​ What technologies can you think of that would alleviate the pain of having to spend countless hours locating your lost pet.​ ​

Scenario 9 - Trees after storm

With the weather patterns drastically having changed over the years, typhoons have become quite rampant and this year, Hong Kong was massively impacted. As a solutions provider, the city mayor is concerned on the impact of the disaster on the forest ecosystem. ​ Could you help develop a technology that would provide data on how the indigenous trees are affected after the storm.​ ​

Scenario 10 - Wild fire solutions

Wild fires have become common place especially in Europe and South America. ​ What technological solutions could you provide to help mitigate causes and alleviate the impact in has on the ecosystem.​ ​

Scenario 11 - Good Mental Health

We are the digital natives, living in an era of unprecedented growth in the entertainment industry. The digital natives are the future of tomorrow and that future must be safe guarded by having a lifestyle which puts emphasis on wellbeing. ​ Could you explore developing technologies that will improve the overall well being of young adults over the next 10 years.​ ​

Scenario 12 - Minimized Food Waste

Food waste is a global problem especially in the developed nations. It is even a bigger problem in our international schools. ​ Could you think of technologies which could be deployed to minimize food wastage in schools.​ ​
